Privacy Policy — GoBag+ Last updated: March 8, 2026 1. Overview GoBag+ ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties. 2. Data Storage All data you enter in the App (Go Bag items, home supplies, emergency contacts, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. You are in full control of your data at all times. 3. iCloud Sync (Premium Feature) If you enable Family Sharing, your data is synced via Apple CloudKit — Apple's private cloud infrastructure. This data is associated with your Apple ID and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. GoBag+ does not have access to your iCloud data. 4. Notifications The App may send local push notifications to remind you about expiring items or low stock. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server. 5. Subscriptions Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. GoBag+ does not collect or store any payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple and governed by their privacy policy. 6. Analytics & Tracking GoBag+ does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites. 7. Data We Do Not Collect * Name, email, or contact information * Location data * Device identifiers * Usage analytics * Crash reports sent to third parties 8. Children's Privacy The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy. 10. Contact If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: iwfyew@gmail.com
